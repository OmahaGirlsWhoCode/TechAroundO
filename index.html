What is Tech Around O!?

Tech Around O! is the baby of the inaugural Omaha Girls Who Code high school group. It's goal is to serve the youth of Omaha with a glimpse into the local tech scene. You can explore profiles of people involved in tech, search for Omaha tech hot spots, play some awesome tech games, or even read about us, the creators of the site! So go and see the Tech Around O!
